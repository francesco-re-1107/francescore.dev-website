Mi Band Maps

Receive Google Maps directions to your Mi Band 5/4/3, Amazfit Bip or Amazfit Cor



Features

This app lets you receive Google Maps directions directly to your wearable device.

Works everywhere

Mi Band Maps will work wherever you are, no matter if you're abroad or in your own country.

Advanced customization

The notifications are highly customizable. For example, you can choose to receive directions only when walking and only at certain distances. The content of the notifications is also customizable, you may want to receive only direction arrow and the arrive time, for example.

Updates

Mi Band Maps is constantly updated with new features and improvements.
