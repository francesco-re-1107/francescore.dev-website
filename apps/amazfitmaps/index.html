Amazfit Maps

Receive Google Maps directions to your Amazfit smartwatch



Features

This app lets you receive Google Maps directions to your Amazfit smartwatch

Works everywhere

Amazfit Maps will work wherever you are, no matter if you're abroad or in your own country.

Advanced customization

The notifications are highly customizable. For example, you can choose to receive directions only when walking and only at certain distances. The content of the notifications is also customizable, you may want to receive only direction and the arrive time, for example.

Updates

Amazfit Maps is constantly updated with new features and improvements.

Compatible with

  • Amazfit Pace
  • Amazfit Stratos (1, 2, 3)
  • Amazfit Verge
  • Amazfit Verge Lite
  • Amazfit GTR (1, 2, 2e, 3, 3 Pro)
  • Amazfit GTS (1, 2, 2e, 2mini, 3)
